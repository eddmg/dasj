ABOUT RUSSELL

An industry leader.

With over four decades of experience researching and selecting investment managers, Russell is a global financial services organization offering capital markets insights, manager research, portfolio construction, portfolio implementation and indexes. The firm has grown its client footprint to serve investment organizations, institutional investors, financial advisors and individuals. Russell has relationships with more than 2,200 investment firms around the world, and currently has $2.6 trillion in assets under advisement. It is from this robust investment backdrop that the Russell Indexes were conceived. Russell’s Indexes business began in 1984 to accurately measure U.S. market segments and track market performance for Russell’s investment management and consulting businesses.

Today, Russell Indexes are used to benchmark more than $4.1 trillion in investor assets. In fact, more institutional funds track Russell Indexes than all other U.S. equity indexes combined.1 Russell‘s index data is also used to create a variety of exchange traded products offered through some of the best-known firms in the industry, representing more than $85 billion in assets. Russell Indexes strives to combine its rigorous index construction methodology with best of class thinking to be a trusted source of index intelligence.
