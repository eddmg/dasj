RUSSELL INDEXES AT A GLANCE

An acccurate and comprehensive approach.

Objective, consistent, reliable and accurate. All are attributes you expect from the investment industry standard set by U.S. Russell indexes, and all are part of the Global Indexes. At Russell, we adapt our index design to reflect ever-evolving markets, creating indexes that are truly relevant within the current global landscape. We use a single market cap break to determine which global companies are considered large cap, and which small cap. We then apply a modular methodology for segmenting 100% of the U.S. equity investable universe with no gaps or overlaps. Our best known U.S. index series is composed of distinct, market asset classes, additionally allocated into growth and value styles. As investor needs have evolved, so too have our indexes — which have become the foundation for a variety of new index strategies.
